What is the project goal?

We are building an app that will manage trees that are planted around the country. Our solution will provide the user with a system that will be able to view tree locations on an interactive map, manage staff, manage trees and their state, and then provide the non profit org a way to spread their cause online.

How will we acomplish this goal?

My Team and I have decided to use Angular and Firebase for our project. The decision was easy since firebase offers amazing hosting and services, like a integrated Database solution, all for free.

Cloud firestore (firebase's database solution) Was probably the biggest factor to why we chose firebase over our initial solution Heroku. Heroku is an amazing PaaS but everything they offered their users was cut back to ensure users buy the entry level packadge. This is not the case with firebase, whick gives you almost everythiing they could possibly offer. This is becuase google knows the real money lies in bigger companys, so they want to lower the entry barrier with firebase's entry level plan to then build an ecosystem and charge you when your company exceeds a certain threshhold.

Firebase Hosting (firebase's hosting solution) was the second biggest factor to why we chose firebase over our initial solution Heroku. Heroku offered hosting, but put your app offline as soon as it was inactive for 15 min. Firebase offerers not only full hosting, but also access to https and their CDN caching servers worldwide.

Angular was ranked the second most used technology after Node.js in the 2018 Stack Overflow survey. The reasons for such a high rank are clear: this frontend tool is equipped with robust components to help developers write readable, maintainable, and easy-to-use code. And since its owned by google it has AMAZING integration with firebase.
And thats probably the biggest factor that made the choice for us
